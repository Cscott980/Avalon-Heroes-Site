Drag & Drop to Equip
Click and hold any weapon or armor in your bag, then drag it to the matching equipment slot — or drag it directly onto your character to auto-equip. You can also drag items between bag slots to reorganize, and drag equipped gear back to your bag to unequip it.
Stats

Your hero has five core stats. Each piece of armor and every weapon you equip can raise these values — stronger gear means a stronger hero. Check the Stats Guide button inside the game to see your current numbers.

STR
Strength
Increases the damage dealt by your weapons. More STR = harder hits.
VIT
Vitality
Increases your maximum health (HP). More VIT = harder to kill.

Beginner Tips
1
Check your inventory first. You start with free starter gear — equip it before engaging enemies. Press I and drag your weapon and armor pieces into their equipment slots.
2
Unsheath your weapon before fighting. Press Z to draw your weapon. You won't be able to attack while it's sheathed!
3
Keep moving. Standing still in Horde Mode is a fast way to get surrounded. Use W A S D to stay on the move and avoid getting cornered.
4
Prioritize armor upgrades. Vitality (VIT) from armor keeps you alive longer. Drag heavier armor pieces into your equipment slots whenever you find them.
5
It's a demo — bugs happen. If something feels off, use the Feedback button in the game bar to let the team know. Every report helps shape the final game!
